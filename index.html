My name is Suwan Cho, and I am junior in Architecture.
My design work often focuses on clarity, structure, and relationship
between people and spaces they occupy. Studying abroad in the United States
has helped me develop a broader perspective on architectural culture and design approaches.
I am particularly drawn to architects such as Shigeru Ban and Kengo Kuma for their
ability to balance structure, expression and experimentation.
Overall, I aim to create architecture that is thoughtful, spatially clear,
and grounded in real use rather than purely formal gestures.
I am currently seeking an internship where I can continue developing my design skills
and gain hands-on experience in a real architecture field.
